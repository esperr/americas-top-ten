About America's Top Ten Bills

Why America's Top Ten Bills?

America's Top Ten Bills is a remixing of the data found at the Most Viewed Bills page that enhances that list in a couple of useful ways. It not only shows the sponsor of each measure, but also gives a readout of its past performance on the "chart" by detailing its previous week rank, peak rank and total number of weeks in the top 10.

Technology and Credits

All data comes from the friendly folks at congress.gov. There's not presently an API for extractng this information, so this application relies upon screen-scraping using beautifulsoup. Visit https://github.com/esperr/top-ten-bill-scraper for the scraper's code.

Responsive design made less painful with Bootstrap. Find the source for this application at https://github.com/esperr/americas-top-ten.
